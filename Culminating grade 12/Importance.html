There are MANY computer languages for different purposes. C and C++ remain the fastest to execute and are used by the gaming industry, for instance. Learning a new language is like learning how to program, you must learn to express yourself within the rules of the language. There is a grammar to comprehend. And what you write must be interpretable by another human being, whereas, in the world of programming, you are telling the computer exactly what to do.
